Welcome to Tobacco Cessation

Online Tobacco Training and Resources for Dental Professionals

Helping patients who want to quit tobacco use is a critical element of dental practice. This self-paced, online course is designed to teach dentists and dentists-in-training the basic concepts of evidence-based tobacco cessation pharmacotherapy and behavioral counseling. After completing this course you will be equipped with the knowledge and skills to effectively assess and treat tobacco use.

Tobacco Cessation was developed in collaboration by partners from the Columbia University College of Dental Medicine and Center for New Media Teaching and Learning. The program was funded through support from the Triangle Initiative and Pfizer Foundation.
